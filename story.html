Our Story

RC's NYC Pizza & Pasta is built on a simple idea: bring real, no-shortcuts pizza to Texas. We're talking the kind of pie you'd find in a corner slice shop in New York—thin, foldable, with the right chew and char—plus the regional styles that make American pizza so good.

We don't stick to one style. Our menu includes New York thin crust (hand-tossed, the classic), Sicilian (thick, square, pan-baked), New Haven–style (charred and thin), and Chicago tavern style (thin, crisp, cut in squares). And yes, we do pasta too—because sometimes you want a plate of spaghetti or a sub just as much as a slice.

We're proud to serve Kingwood and The Woodlands from two locations. Whether you're in for a quick slice, a full pie, or a family dinner, we're here to deliver the authenticity and quality we believe in.

Come in, order online for pickup or delivery, and taste the difference.
