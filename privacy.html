Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

RC's NYC Pizza & Pasta ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we may collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and related services.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect information that your browser sends, such as your IP address, device type, and pages visited. When you place an order through our online ordering system (e.g., Toast), that service and we may collect your name, phone number, email, delivery address, and payment information as needed to fulfill your order.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your order or inquiries
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with applicable law

Third-Party Services

Our website may link to third-party services (e.g., online ordering, maps). Those services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices. We encourage you to read their policies when you leave our site or use their services.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality (e.g., preferences). Third-party ordering or analytics services may use their own cookies. You can adjust your browser settings to limit or block cookies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Online payments are processed by our ordering provider; we do not store full payment card details on our servers.

Your Rights

You may contact us to ask about the data we hold about you or to request correction or deletion, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
